What is Short Trading?

Betting price will FALL. Profit when price drops, lose when it rises.

Example: Short ETH at $2,000 Price drops to $1,900 = $100 profit per ETH Price rises to $2,100 = $100 loss per ETH With 5x leverage: $100 move = $500 profit/loss on $100 capital
‚ö†Ô∏è Leverage Reality Check:
‚Ä¢ 5x leverage: Need 20% adverse move for liquidation
‚Ä¢ 10x leverage: Need 10% adverse move
‚Ä¢ 20x leverage: Need 5% adverse move
‚Ä¢ 50x leverage: Need 2% adverse move (liquidation almost certain)

Beginners should use 2x-5x MAXIMUM.
